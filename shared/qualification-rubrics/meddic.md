# MEDDIC — qualification rubric variant

Use this file's content to replace `shared/qualification-rubric.md` if your team uses MEDDIC (Metrics / Economic buyer / Decision criteria / Decision process / Identify pain / Champion) — the original MEDDPICC predecessor without Paper Process or Competition.

## Pillars

| Pillar | 0 — Missing | 1 — Partial | 2 — Validated |
|---|---|---|---|
| **M — Metrics** | No quantified business impact mentioned. | Generic value claim with no number. | Specific $ / % / time metric tied to a baseline. |
| **E — Economic buyer** | Unknown or not identified. | Name known, never met. | Met live; confirmed they sign off. |
| **D — Decision criteria** | No criteria captured. | Vague preferences. | Written/spoken list of weighted criteria. |
| **D — Decision process** | Unknown. | Some steps known, no map. | Mapped stages, owners, dates, current step. |
| **I — Identify pain** | Pain unclear. | Pain named, no cost-of-inaction. | Specific pain + cost + emotional weight. |
| **C — Champion** | No champion. | Coach, no advocacy proof. | Has fought for us, can name our differentiators. |

**Total**: 0–12. Bands:

- **0–4** — Early discovery.
- **5–8** — Active selling.
- **9–12** — Closeable.

## Output line format

```
RUBRIC: M:2 E:1 D-crit:2 D-proc:1 I:2 C-hamp:2 | Total: 10/12 | Methodology: MEDDIC
```

## Notes

MEDDIC drops MEDDPICC's Paper Process and Competition pillars. Works for transactional / mid-market deals where procurement and competitive battles are less load-bearing. Add Paper Process when you start losing deals to procurement timelines; add Competition when you start losing to specific named competitors.
